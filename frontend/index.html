How It Works

  1. 1
    Parse

    PDF text is extracted page by page.

  2. 2
    Chunk

    Text is split into overlapping 600-char windows.

  3. 3
    Embed

    Each chunk is encoded into a 384-dim vector.

  4. 4
    Store

    Vectors are persisted in ChromaDB with cosine space.

  5. 5
    Retrieve & Generate

    Your question is embedded → top chunks retrieved → Claude answers.
